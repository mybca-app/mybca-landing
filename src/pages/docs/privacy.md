# myBCA Privacy Policy

## Personal data

myBCA does not collect any personally identifiable information. No personally identifiable information is shared with third parties.

## Request logging

In order to help us improve our services and ensure the security of our platforms, myBCA's backend services may log requests. Request logs may include:

* Date and time of the request
* User agent string
* Origin IP address
* Destination URL/endpoint

## Third-party services

myBCA makes use of third-party services to provide certain infrastructure and functionality.

* **Firebase Cloud Messaging:** myBCA uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to send notifications for
	buses you have starred. When you star a bus on one of our mobile apps, your device ID is associated
	with that bus so that you receive relevant and timely notifications.
* **Cloudflare:** myBCA uses Cloudflare to provide CDN, hosting, and platform security
	services. Cloudflare may process data related to your request. For details as to which
	specific data is processed, please review
	<a href="https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Cloudflare's privacy policy</a>.
