# Frequently Asked Questions

## My club has an event. Can you add it to the in-app event calendar?

Please open the Events tab in myBCA and click the link to view the event submission form. You must log in with your school account to fill out the form. Pay close attention to the requirements stated in the form.

## How do notifications work?

Notifications are only available on our iOS and Android apps. In order to use notification functionality, you must enable notifications when prompted upon first launching myBCA.

When you have notifications enabled and one of your starred buses arrives, you will receive a notification with its location.

## What is the logo design based on?

The myBCA logo design is an outline of the main BCA building when viewed from satellite imagery.
